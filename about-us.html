Our Story

A homegrown agency
going global.

At Ecom Deals, we specialize in end-to-end e-commerce solutions — building tailored growth strategies, turning under-performing investments into profitable storefronts, and running comprehensive training programs for the next wave of online sellers.

We started in Rawalpindi in 2023 with a single goal: prove that operators based in Pakistan could compete — and win — against the biggest agencies in the world. Eighteen months later we had opened a second office in Wah Cantt, expanded into Lahore within the same year, and in 2025 launched our southern operations from Karachi.

Our work doesn't stop at the border. From these four offices we run live storefronts and full marketing operations for clients across the United Kingdom, the United States and the Middle East — official partners of TikTok Shop, TEMU UK, Shopify, Meta and Google.
